🧾 DSS Defense Center

If DSS/CPS has stepped into your life — you’re not powerless. This page is your first shield in defending yourself and your family. We’re not attorneys, but we’ve been in the fight, and we’re here to equip you with strategy, documents, and bold truth.

🚨 If They Show Up at Your Door:

  • You have the right to remain silent. You do NOT have to let them in without a warrant.
  • Ask for all allegations in writing — don’t speak off the record.
  • Record the interaction if legal in your state (check this guide).

🧠 Know Your Rights:

  • You have the right to an attorney before signing anything.
  • You can refuse entry without a court order or exigent circumstances.
  • You are not obligated to take drug tests on the spot. Request it through your attorney or court.
