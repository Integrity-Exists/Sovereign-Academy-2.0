Reopen Your Case

If your case ended unfairly — if your attorney failed, new evidence came up, or you were never properly heard — you have options. We’ll help you reopen your case and demand the justice you were denied.

🔍

    ⚖️ Legal Tools to Reopen a Case

    • Motion to Set Aside or Vacate Judgment – Used when there was fraud, lack of notice, or procedural error
    • Motion for New Trial – Based on ineffective counsel or newly discovered evidence
    • Appeal – Challenge the decision in a higher court (if you're within the time limit)

    📝 Example Wording

    “My attorney failed to present critical evidence. I only discovered after judgment that [describe evidence or violation]. 
I request this case be reopened in the interest of justice.”
