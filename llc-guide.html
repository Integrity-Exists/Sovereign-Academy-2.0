🚀 Start Your LLC — The National Sovereign Way

Protect yourself. Build power. Create legacy.



📌 What is an LLC?

A Limited Liability Company (LLC) legally separates YOU from your business. That means if your business gets sued, your house, car, and personal bank account stay protected.

  • ✅ Easy to start and manage
  • ✅ Accepted in all 50 states
  • ✅ Flexible for single owners or groups
  • ✅ Great for legacy and trust building

🧩 How to Start Your LLC (Nationwide)

  1. Pick a Name: Must be unique in your state. Search on your Secretary of State's website.
  2. Choose a Registered Agent: This can be you or a service that accepts mail on your behalf.
  3. File Articles of Organization: Usually costs $50–$150, varies by state. File online or by mail.
  4. Get an EIN: Free at irs.gov/ein. Required to open a bank account.
  5. Create an Operating Agreement: This tells the world how your business runs (even if it’s just you).
  6. Stay Compliant: Pay your annual fee/tax. Some states require an annual report.

💡 Tips for Sovereign Entrepreneurs

  • ✅ Use a PO Box or virtual address for privacy.
  • ✅ Choose a name that can grow: “Holdings,” “Solutions,” “Group,” etc.
  • ✅ Don’t mix business and personal money — ever.
