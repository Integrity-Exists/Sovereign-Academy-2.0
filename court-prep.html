📝 Before Court

  • Gather and organize all evidence and paperwork.
  • Prepare a short outline of key points you want to make.
  • Practice speaking clearly and calmly.

🧾 Day of Court Checklist

📄 Download Checklist (PDF)
  • Dress professionally and arrive early.
