🗽 Your Civil Liberties

These rights weren’t given by the government. They were recognized by the Constitution to protect you from it.

🔓 First Amendment: Freedom of Speech, Religion, Press & Assembly

You have the right to speak your truth, pray how you want, and organize peacefully — even if it makes others uncomfortable.

🔍 Fourth Amendment: No Unlawful Searches or Seizures

No warrant? No entry. No probable cause? No search. Assert your right to privacy every time.

⚖️ Fifth & Fourteenth Amendments: Due Process

You are innocent until proven guilty. The government cannot deprive you of life, liberty, or property without fair legal steps.

💥 When Rights Are Violated

It’s not just unfair — it’s actionable. Document it. Stand your ground. Sue if necessary. Remedies live here.
