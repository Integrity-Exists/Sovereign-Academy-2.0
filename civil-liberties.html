🗽 Your Civil Liberties

These rights weren’t given by the government. They were recognized by the Constitution to protect you from it.

🔓 First Amendment: Freedom of Speech, Religion, Press & Assembly

You have the right to speak your truth, worship freely, publish ideas, and gather peacefully — even if others don’t like it. That’s real power.

🔍 Fourth Amendment: No Unlawful Searches or Seizures

No warrant? No entry. No probable cause? No search. If they don’t have clear legal authority, you do not have to comply. Period.

⚖️ Fifth & Fourteenth Amendments: Due Process

You are presumed innocent. You are entitled to a fair procedure before any deprivation of life, liberty, or property. That means hearings, notice, and a chance to speak.

💥 When Rights Are Violated

This isn’t just morally wrong — it’s legally actionable. Document everything. File complaints. Use Remedies. Sue. Let the system know you won’t go quietly.
