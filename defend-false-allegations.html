Defend Against False CPS Allegations

You’re not alone. False CPS claims are more common than people think — and more terrifying than most can imagine. But truth and strategy are stronger than fear. Here's how to fight false allegations and win back your life.

🚨 Step-by-Step Defense

  1. Demand Discovery: Get every piece of evidence DSS/CPS has (or claims to have)
  2. Get the Reporter ID: Request the identity or affidavit of the person who made the claim
  3. Challenge the Basis: File a motion if the removal or action was based on hearsay, opinion, or no actual harm
  4. Collect Counter-Evidence: Witness statements, clean drug tests, school letters, text history, photos
  5. Demand an Emergency Hearing: If your child was removed without cause or proof

💥 Sample Statement

“Your Honor, I am the victim of a false allegation. There is no evidence of abuse or neglect, and I have provided documentation to show my innocence. I respectfully request that the court dismiss these actions immediately.”
