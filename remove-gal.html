How to Remove a Guardian ad Litem (GAL)

A GAL is supposed to act in the best interest of the child ‚Äî not the court, not DSS, not one parent. If a GAL is biased, unresponsive, or working against you with a clear conflict of interest, you have the right to file for their removal.
