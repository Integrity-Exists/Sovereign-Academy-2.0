Estate & Property Rights

Understanding your rights when it comes to your property and estate can empower you to protect what’s yours. Whether you're facing code enforcement, eviction threats, or planning for the future, here's what you need to know:

  • Property Ownership: Know the difference between titles, deeds, and liens.
  • Wills & Estates: Learn how to create legally binding wills and assign beneficiaries.
  • Tenant & Landlord Law: Protect yourself with knowledge of tenant rights and eviction procedures.
  • Inheritance Rules: Understand intestate succession and how probate works.
  • Government Overreach: What to do if you receive demolition, zoning, or condemnation notices.
