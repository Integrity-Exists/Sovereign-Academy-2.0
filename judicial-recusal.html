Motion to Recuse a Judge

Every person has the right to a fair and impartial judge. If your judge is biased, has a conflict of interest, or has acted improperly ‚Äî you can file a motion to recuse (remove) them from your case.

  1. File a Motion to Recuse with a supporting affidavit

  4. Submit it before your next hearing (as early as possible)
  5. Request a hearing on the motion if one is not automatically set
