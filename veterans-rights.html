🎖️ Veterans’ Rights & National Resources

If you served, you deserve support. Here's your national guide to navigating VA benefits, military discharge upgrades, legal rights, and more — without the red tape.

⚖️ Legal Rights for Veterans (Nationwide)

  • ✔️ File VA Disability Claims
  • ✔️ Request Military Discharge Upgrades
  • ✔️ Access Free Legal Aid via VA Legal Services
  • ✔️ Seek Expedited Court Consideration (in many states)

🏥 VA Healthcare & Mental Health

Nationwide VA facilities offer care — but many veterans don’t know how to access it. Here’s how to apply:

  1. Apply online: VA.gov Health Care Enrollment
  2. Call 1-877-222-8387 for help
  3. Find a VA clinic near you: VA Facility Locator
